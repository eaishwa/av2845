The following table shows the gross earnings of every STEAM railroad from which regular weekly or monthly return can be obtained. The first two columns of figures give the gross earnings for the latest week or month, and the last two columns the earnings for the period from July 1 to and including such latest week or month. We add a supplementary statement to show the fiscal year totals of those roads whoso fiscal year does not begin with July, but covers some other period. The returns of the electric railways are brought together separately on a subsequent page.
